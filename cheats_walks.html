  neoseeker - XBOX 360 Game Walkthroughs

neoseeker, although popular for its cheat codes and action replays, also has a few FAQs/Walkthroughs. They are limited to recent releases within the past two or so years.

  Game Anyone? - Video Walkthroughs

Game Anyone? is unique in that it is one of the only websites on the web that provides video walkthroughs. The majority of them can be found disjointedly over Youtube and other video networks, so appreciate what they've done here!

  IGN - XBOX 360 FAQs and Walkthroughs

IGN has their FAQs/Walkthroughs broken down into two sections: by platform + game and by hardware. Users on the site submit walkthroughs for separate parts of games (campaigns, for example).

Some games do have full walkthroughs, but it's entirely dependent on the user who submits.

  IGN - Builds, Loadouts, and Skill Trees

IGN has a relatively new feature on its site, which is built around just a few games at the moment - Elder Scrolls, Call of Duty MW3, and Star Wars: The Old Republic. You can view others' build orders, skill tree plans, and loadouts.
