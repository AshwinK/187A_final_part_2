  XBOX 360 Blogs

XBOX 360 has its own blogs under the Social section of the main website. You can view threads by most recently posted, most views, and most comments. This blog is more organized than a lot of the other ones floating around the interet.

  That Videogame BLog

Welcome to what's probably the coolest videogame blog in existence! While other blogs focus on walkthroughs and fail at successfully vlogging (because walkthroughs requires no presence on camera), the guys who run this excel at it.

  Next Gen Walkthroughs Blog

Next Gen Walkthroughs on Youtube is one of the closest entities you'll find to a video game blog channel. Although the staff has recently been focusing on more walkthroughs for a wider variety of games, they still do post normal "vlogs."
