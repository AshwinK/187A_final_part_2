Can't decide what games to buy? Check out XBOX's reviews for all of the top games for Xbox 360. Each game is scored out of five stars and incldues a count of how many people have voted/reviewed.

  Wikipedia - List of XBOX 360 Games

Wikipedia, our best friend on the internet, keeps a list of released XBOX 360 games. Clicking on any game will take you straight to its home page, where you can find detailed reviews, audience reception, and other interesting information.

Keep in mind that these reviews are compiled from elsewhere on the internet and aren't the first-person views of professional critics.
