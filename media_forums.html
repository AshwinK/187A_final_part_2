  TeamXbox Forums

TeamXbox forums is powered by IGN and currently boasts 503,864 Threads, 12,161,466 Posts, and 269,707 Members. It's a huge community that covers every single subject that could be anything moderately related to XBOX 360.

  XBOX 360 Forum Directory


XBOX 360 has its own forum that owners can participate on. Directory sections include General, XBOX Live, XBOX Support, XBOX Live Indie Games, XBOX Live Arcade, and XBOX 360 Games.

  GameFAQs XBOX 360 Forum

GameFAQs' forum/board is a little hard for new users to use, but users who have been on it for a few years will know how to navigate around easily. There are currently over 1000 pages of messages and threads, so searching a query about what you're looking for will most likely return a result.
