This E-Zine was created by Zishan Budhwani and Ashwin Khurana in COGS 187A, March 2012. The purpose of this E-Zine is to help XBOX gamers access the best online resources for gaming without having to scour the web on their own, wasting much valuable gaming time!

As kids, we spent so much time playing games, reading reviews, finding out where the best deals were, and more. We felt that making this E-Zine would help fellow gamers gain access to more games and enable them to contribute to the community more.
